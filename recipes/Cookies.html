Chocolate chip cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies that are also great for dessert!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Mix the butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla
  2. Add baking soda in hot water to the mixture
  3. Add the flour and chocolate chips
  4. Bake in the oven until the edges are golden brown
