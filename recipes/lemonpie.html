Lemon meringue pie

Description

A Lemon meringue pie that's great for dessert

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Lemons
  • Eggs
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Squeeze the lemon and add the eggs and sugar to a bowl
  2. Whisk it well until it haves the desired texture
  3. Add the mixture to the pie crust
  4. Bake in the oven
