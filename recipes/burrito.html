Chicken Burrito

Description

Delicious chicken burrito with lettuce, good for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Tortilla
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Sear the chicken in a pan until it is golden brown
  2. Add the tortilla to the pan
  3. Heat the tortilla and add the chicken
  4. Finish by adding a bunch of lettuce
